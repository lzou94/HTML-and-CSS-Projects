Why Cats are Assholes

Cats. Fluffy, furry, and sometimes affectionate. Although, most cat owners can agree that cats are mostly just assholes. Whether its knocking over cups or killing innocent plants, cats tend to just do whatever they want. It's almost as if the world made dogs to be man's best friend while they made cat's to be man's biggest annoyance. Yet somehow, we still keep them as pets because even though they frustrate the hell out of us, there's just something endearing about how they treat us. Today we will be exploring the reasons as to why cats are just assholes.
